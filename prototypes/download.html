How to install

1

Tap Download for Android above and wait for the file to finish downloading.

2

Open the downloaded ReformedChurch.apk file from your notifications or Downloads folder.

3

If prompted, tap Settings and enable "Install Unknown Apps" for your browser, then go back.

4

Tap Install — the app will appear on your home screen within seconds.
