Church App Design Prototype

Editorial sanctuary, not another template.

This direction treats the app like a beautiful church bulletin reimagined for mobile. The visual language is warm, reverent, tactile, and premium: parchment light, stained-glass color, serious typography, and layered cards that feel collected rather than mass-produced.

Design Direction

The memorable thing here is the contrast: sacred editorial calm with modern product clarity. Large serif headlines carry the emotional weight, while the UI chrome stays restrained and polished.

Tone Warm Light-first, tactile, ceremonial.
Layout Layered Cards overlap softly with asymmetry.
Memory Distinct Feels like a living parish publication.
